What is UltraChrome?

UltraChrome is an open-source, NA?SA backed, web browser like Google Chrome, but it is much faster, more RAM friendly, and faster. In short, it is superior to chrome By Far.

UltraChrome 1.3 will take at most 1ms extra for each 1.5x in size.

The beta_1.5 versions are working on loading pages in O(1/n) time -- the larger the page, the faster it will load.

Roadmap

The upcoming 1.5 release (currently in beta) has an ambitious goal of loading pages in O(1/n) time, meaning that loading time scales inversely with page size; the larger the page, the faster it will load.Our goal is to release it (tm)Soontm-1.

While other companies schedule their releases for Soontm without a second thought, our methodology will involve derivation from the current build, which we promised at Soontm.

The derivation of O(log(n)) runtime will give us the long-coveted O(1/n) runtime.
